We learn. We build. We ship.

DETOVA Labs is a student-led innovation lab from CHRISLAND University. We bring together developers, designers, and storytellers to build Web2 and Web3 solutions that matter. From hackathons to client projects, every line of code and every design is proof of what young talent can do.

About Us

At Detova Labs, we believe students are not the future of innovation, we are already shaping it.

We are a collective of developers, creatives, and researchers building like an agency while staying true to our roots as students hungry for impact. Our mission is simple: create projects that solve problems, grow communities, and inspire trust in young African talent.

We cover:

  • Web2 and Web3 development
  • UI/UX and branding
  • Community building and growth
  • Research, storytelling, and pitching
  • Project management

We are not waiting for permission. We are building the future now.

Internships

DETOVA Labs is more than a lab. We are a launchpad for young talent. Our internship program gives students the chance to step outside the classroom and into real projects.

As an intern you will:

  • Join hackathon teams and client projects
  • Work side-by-side with developers, designers, and storytellers
  • Learn pitching, designing, coding, and growing like a startup team
  • Build a portfolio of real proof-of-work
  • Receive a Detova Labs Certificate of Experience

This is not theory. This is not about waitingfor the future. It is about shaping it.
